ControllerGUI a GUI substitute for a midi contoller


ControllerGUI.ar(numContr, right, top)


A GUI substitute for a midi controller. 


numContr - number of colums

right - window position from right to left in pixels

top - window position from bottom to top in pixels


Example


c = ControllerGUI(1) //number of columns, right, top


c.slider(1, {arg val; (val + 1000).postln});


c.knob(1, {arg val; (val + 1000).postln});




c = ControllerGUI(30,30,400) //number of columns, right, top


c.slider(1, {arg val; "slider1: ".post; val.postln});


c.knob(1, {arg val; "knob1: ".post; val.postln});



c.setSlider(1, 20);


c.setKnob(1, 88);



c.setSlider(10, 20);


c.setKnob(10, 88);


c.setAllSliders(0); //set all sliders at 0


c.setAllKnobs(0); //set all knobs at 0


//moving knobs and sliders with a routine


r = Routine({inf.do({

c.setSlider(rrand(1,30), rrand(0,127));

0.01.yield;

c.setKnob(rrand(1,30), rrand(0,127));

0.01.yield;

})}).play(AppClock);


r.stop; //stop routine


c.close; //close window


/////////////


//lots of knobs and sliders


(

~cont1 = ControllerGUI(34,8,600); //number of columns, right, top
